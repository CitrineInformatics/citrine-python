[ALPHA] a response to a job status check.

The JobStatusResponse summarizes the status for the entire job.

Parameters

  • job_type (str) – the type of job for this status report

  • status (str) – the actual status of the job. One of “Submitted”, “Pending”, “Running”, “Success”, or “Failure”.

  • tasks (List[TaskNode]) – all of the constituent task required to complete this job
