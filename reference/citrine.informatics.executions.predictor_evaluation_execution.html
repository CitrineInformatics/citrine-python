class citrine.informatics.executions.predictor_evaluation_execution.PredictorEvaluationExecution¶

Bases: citrine._rest.resource.Resource[PredictorEvaluationExecution], citrine._rest.asynchronous_object.AsynchronousObject

The execution of a PredictorEvaluationWorkflow.

Possible statuses are INPROGRESS, SUCCEEDED, and FAILED. Predictor evaluation executions also have a status_description field with more information.
